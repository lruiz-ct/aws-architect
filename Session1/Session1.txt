Data Center
	A data center is a building filled with computers

Availability Zone
	An availability zone may be several data centers, but because they are close togheter, they are counted as 1 availability zone

Region
	A Region is a geographical area. Each region consist of 2 (or more) Availability zones

Edge Location
	Edge locations are endpoints for AWS  that are used for caching content. Typically used on AWS Cloud Front (CDN)

https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/global-infrastructure/regions_az/
